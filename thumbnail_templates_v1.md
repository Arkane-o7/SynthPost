# SynthPost Thumbnail Templates v1

These templates are original SynthPost layouts. They borrow broad thumbnail mechanics from the research - clarity, stakes, faces, logos, maps, money numbers - but use a distinct SynthPost identity: dark editorial backgrounds, cyan/gold/red accent systems, analytical framing, and restrained logo usage.

Each template should become a Remotion/React component with typed props.

## Template 1: Authority Warning

Best use case: CEO, founder, regulator, minister, or expert warns about a major AI/business consequence.

Layout description:

- Large portrait on left or right, 45-58 percent canvas height.
- Dark impact scene behind: robots, factory, data center, office floor, market chart.
- Headline block in opposite upper quadrant.
- Small source/analysis tag near bottom-left.

Required assets:

- One high-quality person image.
- One contextual background.
- Optional company logo.

Text placement:

- Main headline in upper-left or upper-right, 3-5 words.
- Optional small tag: `AI BRIEFING`, `MARKET SIGNAL`, or source label.

Color treatment:

- Graphite base.
- Red accent for danger, cyan for analytical warning.
- Subtle rim light on subject.

Example headline:

- "Nadella says AI agents will reshape white-collar work."

Example thumbnail text:

- `SATYA'S AI WARNING`

Why it works:

- Authority creates credibility; background creates scale; short warning phrase opens the curiosity gap.

## Template 2: Global AI Faceoff

Best use case: Country vs country, company vs company, founder vs regulator, model vs model.

Layout description:

- Two opposing faces or logos on left/right.
- Center divider line, route, lightning seam, or data axis.
- Optional map or metric in the center.
- Headline spans top or lower third on a dark plate.

Required assets:

- Two faces or two logos.
- Optional map/flag/company background.

Text placement:

- Main text across top or bottom.
- `VS` only when true conflict exists.

Color treatment:

- Blue/cyan side vs red/gold side.
- Avoid chaotic lightning unless model competition is the topic.

Example headline:

- "Google's AI data hub faces a local revolt."

Example thumbnail text:

- `FARMERS VS GOOGLE`

Why it works:

- Direct opposition makes a complex policy or business story instantly legible.

## Template 3: Sovereign AI Stack

Best use case: India AI infrastructure, government AI, local models, compute sovereignty, national tech strategy.

Layout description:

- Dark India/world map as background.
- Server racks, chip, language grid, or model icon layered over map.
- One leader/founder/person on one side.
- Compact headline in top-left or lower-left.

Required assets:

- Map layer.
- One logo or symbolic AI object.
- Optional person image.

Text placement:

- 3-5 word claim in a dark or frosted title plate.

Color treatment:

- Cyan/blue base with gold or saffron accent.
- Use red only for dependency risk or bans.

Example headline:

- "India races to build sovereign AI compute."

Example thumbnail text:

- `INDIA'S AI STACK`

Why it works:

- It turns abstract sovereignty into visible infrastructure.

## Template 4: Money Deal Bomb

Best use case: Funding round, IPO, acquisition, capex, export number, valuation, government deal.

Layout description:

- Oversized money number as primary visual.
- Company logo or founder portrait secondary.
- Background shows deal context: exchange, data center, factory, boardroom, chip plant.
- One proof badge: `DEAL`, `IPO`, `EXPORTS`, `CAPEX`.

Required assets:

- One number from brief.
- Company logo.
- Optional founder or facility image.

Text placement:

- Money number large, center-left or lower-third.
- Short claim above or beside it.

Color treatment:

- Gold/green accent.
- Dark background for premium contrast.

Example headline:

- "A $40 billion compute buildout changes India's AI race."

Example thumbnail text:

- `$40B COMPUTE RACE`

Why it works:

- Money numbers are easy to understand and create scale instantly.

## Template 5: Map Crisis Marker

Best use case: Geopolitical conflict, regulation, supply-chain shift, country rankings, regional bans.

Layout description:

- Full-bleed map or satellite-style background.
- One glowing marker, route, or blocked area.
- One leader/company/logo cutout.
- Headline in a corner card.

Required assets:

- Map layer.
- One main person/logo/object.
- Optional flag colors as subtle accents.

Text placement:

- Main text on dark plate, left side preferred.
- Keep map labels minimal.

Color treatment:

- Red for crisis, green for boom, cyan for tech route.

Example headline:

- "India overtakes South Korea in a key manufacturing ranking."

Example thumbnail text:

- `INDIA HITS TOP 5`

Why it works:

- Maps make global stories feel concrete without needing many words.

## Template 6: Factory Boom

Best use case: Manufacturing, electronics, chips, data centers, exports, jobs, supply-chain stories.

Layout description:

- Factory/data-center/chip plant background.
- Product row, robot arm, server aisle, or chip macro in foreground.
- Leader/founder portrait on one side.
- Main number or ranking in lower-third.

Required assets:

- Infrastructure background.
- One person or company logo.
- Optional money/export/ranking number.

Text placement:

- Top-left headline or lower-third number.

Color treatment:

- Steel blue and graphite base.
- Green/gold accent for growth.

Example headline:

- "India's electronics exports hit a new high."

Example thumbnail text:

- `THE FACTORY SHIFT`

Why it works:

- Physical scenes make macroeconomic AI-adjacent stories feel real and inspectable.

## Template 7: Logo Collision

Best use case: AI model competition, product rivalry, platform shift, vendor replacement.

Layout description:

- Two or three large logos arranged as conflict cards.
- Optional founder face centered or offset.
- Background: model benchmark grid, terminal, circuit board, or market chart.
- One concise comparison headline.

Required assets:

- Two or three logos.
- Optional person image.

Text placement:

- Top or center headline: `X VS Y`, `X UNDER PRESSURE`, `Y TAKES THE LEAD`.

Color treatment:

- Use each brand's color subtly, unified by dark SynthPost grade.
- Avoid rainbow logo clutter.

Example headline:

- "OpenAI's market lead shrinks as rivals gain share."

Example thumbnail text:

- `OPENAI UNDER PRESSURE`

Why it works:

- Competition is one of the clearest click drivers in AI news.

## Template 8: Device Shock

Best use case: Consumer tech, app launch, model productization, hardware pricing, phone/laptop/chip stories.

Layout description:

- One iconic object centered and huge.
- Person or logo cropped on edge.
- Background shows product context: app UI, money, chip, supply chain, medical scan.
- Headline in top-left or top-center.

Required assets:

- Product/object image or generated symbolic render.
- Optional person/logo.

Text placement:

- 3-4 word claim, no subtitle.

Color treatment:

- Match product category: cyan for AI, gold for pricing, red for warning.

Example headline:

- "AI chip costs could make iPhones more expensive."

Example thumbnail text:

- `IPHONE PRICE SHOCK`

Why it works:

- A single object is highly readable on mobile and expands beyond insider audiences.

## Template 9: Agent Swarm

Best use case: Agentic AI, automation, jobs, AI assistants, workflow platforms, enterprise productivity.

Layout description:

- Repeated AI-agent silhouettes, robot workers, or node grid in background.
- One human authority or company logo foreground.
- Main claim in bold block.

Required assets:

- Agent/robot/grid background.
- One person or logo.

Text placement:

- Large text over a dark rectangle: `100 AGENTS`, `AI JOB SHOCK`, `ONE AGENT, MANY TASKS`.

Color treatment:

- Cyan/blue base.
- Red accents for displacement stories; green for productivity stories.

Example headline:

- "One enterprise agent now controls dozens of workflows."

Example thumbnail text:

- `ONE AGENT, MANY TASKS`

Why it works:

- Repetition visually communicates scale and automation.

## Template 10: Document Exposed

Best use case: Security findings, leaked memo, lawsuit, audit, policy ban, regulatory filing, hidden financials.

Layout description:

- Angled document/screenshot card as central object.
- Red or gold stamp overlay.
- Person/logo in the corner.
- Dark newsroom background.

Required assets:

- Real document/screenshot or stylized generic document if the real file cannot be shown.
- One person/logo.

Text placement:

- Main headline top-left or across document: `MODEL EXPOSED`, `BAN SPARKS RISK`, `THE HIDDEN COST`.

Color treatment:

- Red for risk/exposure.
- Gold for exclusive analysis.

Example headline:

- "A security review found 26 issues in a new AI model."

Example thumbnail text:

- `MODEL RISK EXPOSED`

Why it works:

- Documents imply evidence and insider access, which increases credibility.

## Template 11: Infrastructure Race

Best use case: Compute, cloud, data centers, energy, semiconductors, GPU supply, sovereign infrastructure.

Layout description:

- Wide data-center/server aisle or chip fab as full-bleed background.
- Route lines or capacity bars overlaid.
- Company/country logos arranged as competitors.
- Money/capacity number as proof.

Required assets:

- Data-center/factory background.
- One to three logos.
- Optional map.

Text placement:

- Main text in lower-left or top-left, number large.

Color treatment:

- Deep blue/graphite.
- Cyan grid overlay.
- Gold for capex numbers.

Example headline:

- "New compute capacity changes the AI infrastructure map."

Example thumbnail text:

- `THE COMPUTE RACE`

Why it works:

- It gives a physical form to the hidden infrastructure behind AI.

## Template 12: Inside The Deal

Best use case: Interview, exclusive, partnership, valuation, strategic alliance, founder explanation.

Layout description:

- Two or three people arranged as a clean editorial triangle.
- Company logo or deal object in center.
- Background: office, boardroom, campus, data center, or product screen.
- Small `INSIDE` or `EXPLAINED` tag.

Required assets:

- One to three people.
- One logo/object.
- Optional deal number.

Text placement:

- Main claim across bottom or upper-left.
- Small tag in top-left.

Color treatment:

- Cyan/gold premium grade.
- Avoid red unless the deal is troubled.

Example headline:

- "Why HCL chose Sarvam for enterprise AI."

Example thumbnail text:

- `WHY HCL CHOSE SARVAM`

Why it works:

- It frames a business partnership as a decision with consequences.
